Who am I?

I'm currently a Ph.D. Candidate at Vanderbilt University. I'm a cancer systems biologist studying phenotypic heterogeneity and plasticity in Small Cell Lung Cancer. We think that plasticity of cell identity is a major cause for the inevitable relapse of SCLC tumors, as cells transition to a resistant state that no longer responds to treatment. I'm also interested in single cell analysis, machine learning, and bioinformatics. Besides science, I am obsessed with art, music, and the ocean.
